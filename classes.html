Dance Classes Timetable

  • Ballet Basics

    Beginner-friendly ballet class focusing on posture, balance, and basic movements. Perfect for those new to ballet.

  • Hip-Hop Groove

    High-energy dance class with urban beats and street dance moves. Burn calories while having fun.

  • Contemporary Flow

    A fluid dance class that combines elements of ballet, modern, and jazz. Focuses on expression and movement.

  • Bollywood Beats

    A lively dance class inspired by Bollywood music. Learn energetic choreography and have a blast.

  • Latin Salsa

    Salsa your way into fitness with this fun and rhythmic dance class. No partner required.

  • Jazz Funk

    A mix of jazz dance and street funk. This class is all about attitude, style, and having a great time.

  • Ballroom Basics

    Learn the fundamentals of ballroom dancing, including waltz, tango, and foxtrot. No experience needed.

  • Tap Dance

    Get into the rhythm with this tap dance class. Improve your footwork and coordination.

  • Belly Dance

    Shimmy and shake in this exciting belly dance class. Focuses on core strength and fluid movements.

  • Afrobeat Dance

    Experience the vibrant energy of Afrobeat dance. Move to the rhythm of African beats.
